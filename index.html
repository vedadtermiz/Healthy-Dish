Quinoa Power Salad

A nutritious, protein-packed meal ready in under 30 minutes

Pro Tips

  • Rinse quinoa thoroughly to remove bitter saponins
  • Let the salad rest for 10 minutes after mixing for better flavor
  • Store leftovers in an airtight container for up to 3 days

Step 1: Cook Quinoa

Rinse quinoa and cook with water for 15 minutes. Let it cool.

For fluffier quinoa, use a 1:2 ratio of quinoa to water.

Step 2: Prepare Protein

Cook chicken breast (or tofu) with olive oil until done.

For juicier chicken, let it rest for 5 minutes after cooking.

Step 3: Chop Vegetables

Dice bell pepper and avocado. Wash spinach.

Cut vegetables into similar sizes for better presentation.

Step 4: Combine

Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

Add dressing gradually to prevent over-saturation.
